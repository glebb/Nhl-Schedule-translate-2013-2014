    This tool lists NHL games in your preferred timezone. If you are like me, you are mostly interested in games, which are not showing in the middle of the night (locally) during the work week. What I am interested in is games during the weekend, or games that start early enough for me to watch. By default it will show all upcoming games in Finnish time, which fall inside desired timerange, higlighting games for weekends (when losing a night's sleep is not so bad). If you don't live in Finland, check the list of acceptable timezones from wikipedia.

    As a bonus you can save the game to your favourite calendar by downloading the ical item.
