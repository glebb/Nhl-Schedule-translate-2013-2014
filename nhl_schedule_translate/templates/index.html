with timezones

- End by (required)
- Team

    If you are like me, you live far away from US ET -timezone, which makes it hard to check the schedule for nhl games from NHL site. Also because the time difference is so huge, at least I am mostly interested in games which are not showing in the middle of the night locally. Well, this tool can translate the timezone to what you prefer, and additionally filter the games you are interested in, based on team and on "on-air" -hours. By default it will show games which start between 7:00 and 22:30. If you don't live in Finland, check the list of acceptable timezones from wikipedia.

    As a bonus you can save the game to your favourite calendar by downloading the ical item.
